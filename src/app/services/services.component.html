With our scenic garden of fragrant flowers and ravishing water fountains, our hotel offers the perfect spots for photoshoots. Go to the front desk to book a time slot with photographers to cherish the beauty of nature, Indian style.

Isha Palace provides convenient and timely housekeeping services to ensure all rooms maintain cleanliness and elegance. Rooms are cleaned daily and sheets and towels are changed as well.

Isha Palace provides fresh and tasty food right to your room with the In-Room Dining Service amenity. This amenity allows you to relax and stay in your room while your food is brought to you. This amenity is available at any time at your convenience.
