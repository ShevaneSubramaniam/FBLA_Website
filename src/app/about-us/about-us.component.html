About Isha Palace

Our Story

The name Isha, meaning 'divine one' or 'one who rules', was chosen to reflect the luxury of Isha Palace and the divinity of Indian culture. Our hotel is an Indian-themed hotel. Isha Palace is a luxury hotel based in Atlanta, Georgia. Everything from the architecture to the food is designed by professionals who strive to make the hotel a full Indian experience. We wanted this hotel to be elegant, but also a new experience for most people. That's when we thought about an India-themed hotel that brought the sights and tastes of India right here to Atlanta city. Now you can experience the art, culture, and cuisine of India without going out of the country, and for those who feel a little homesick, a small slice of home is right here.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create lasting memories for our guests by delivering exceptional service and personalized experiences. We aim to be the epitome of hospitality in every way.
